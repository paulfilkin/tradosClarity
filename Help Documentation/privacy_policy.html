tradosClarity Privacy Policy

Effective Date:27 June 2025

Data Collection

tradosClarity does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension stores user preferences (keyboard shortcuts) locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Required to enhance accessibility on Trados Cloud pages
  • storage: Required to save your keyboard shortcut preferences locally
